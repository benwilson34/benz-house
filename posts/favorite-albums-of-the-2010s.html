my 100 favorite albums of the 2010s

published Sat, Dec 28, 2019

Gonna be doing my current top ten albums for each year in this beautiful decade of music. This was a decade when my taste got blown the fxck open many many times as these artists released innovative, imaginative, brave, concise, provocative, or otherwise just striking works.

For the record, I'm counting EPs as albums, and each year's list is in alphabetical order because picking top tens was hard enough on its own 🔤

Anyway here's...

i. Bonobo - Black Sands

ii. Dhafer Youssef Quartet - Festival de Jazz de San Javier

iii. Flying Lotus - Cosmogramma

iv. Maps and Atlases - Perch Patchwork

v. Sleigh Bells - Treats

vi. Teebs & Jackhigh - The Tropics (EP)

vii. Tera Melos - Patagonian Rats

viii. Tobacco - Maniac Meat

ix. TOKiMONSTA - Midnight Menu
